MINECRAFT


Prepare for an adventure of limitless possibilities as you build, mine, battle mobs, and explore the ever-changing Minecraft landscape. If you can dream it, you can build it. Put your imagination and limitless resources to work with Creative Mode. Get crafty and use the surrounding environment to gather building materials—see how breaking down trees can help you create something new. It’s always best to avoid the unpredictable by distancing yourself from wandering mobs—you never know what’ll happen if they get too close! Discover all the versatile ways dust from the Redstone ore can be used to enhance your creations, bring them to life, or give them some bang.
